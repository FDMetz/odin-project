Ham and cheese sandwich

Sandwich

The most consumed sandwich in Argentina, it is always a good time for one of these either toasted or tomato and lettuce.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Condiments

Intructions

  1. Cut the bread in half
  2. Spread the bread slices with mayonnaise or other condiment
  3. Add as many slices of ham and cheese as you like
  4. Optional, Can add tomate slices, letture or another thing, enjoy
