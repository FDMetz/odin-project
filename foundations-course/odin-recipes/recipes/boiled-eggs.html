Boiled egg

Boiled eggs are not only easy and cheap to make, but they are also healthy and filling, an excellent choice if you are short on time.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Pot
  • Water
  • Salt optional

Intructions

  1. Heat water to boiling point
  2. Carefully place the eggs in the cooking pot
  3. Wait seven minutos
  4. Remove the eggs from the pot
  5. Immerse the eggs in cold water for a few minutes
  6. Peel and serve the eggs, if you want you can add salt.
