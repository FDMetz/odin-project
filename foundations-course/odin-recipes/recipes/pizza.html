Pizza

Simple muzzarella pizza

In a few steps you will learn how to make a delicious pizza, this recipe is very simple so feel free to add ingredients of your choice.

Ingredients

  • flour 500g
  • yeast 25g
  • salt 1g
  • water 275cc
  • tomate sause
  • mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Combine the flour, sugar, yeast and water, then knead until a smooth dough is obtained and let it rest until it doubles in size.
  2. Divide the doughs and knead them.
  3. In a bowl pour the tomato sauce and season to taste, spread the sauce over the dough and add the mozzarella.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at two-hundred grades for 30 minutes.
  5. Add oregano before cutting and you can enjoy.
